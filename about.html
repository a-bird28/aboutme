About Me

Hello! My name is Anna, and I am a software engineer in Avi Ma'ayan's lab at the Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai. I develop open-source tools to help experimental biologists easily analyze their data. I care about making complex things simple, whether it's a user-friendly interface, clear visualization, or well-organized demo session.

In my free time I love to design knitwear, listen to as much music as I can, and create new recipes.
