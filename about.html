About Me

Hello! My name is Anna, and I am a bioinformatics software engineer in Avi Ma'ayan's lab at the Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai. I develop open-source tools to help experimental biologists easily analyze their data. My current research focuses on:

  • 1. Constructing and analyzing a human gene regulatory network using ChIP-seq and RNA-seq data
  • 2. Analyzing RNA-seq and proteomics data to predict tumor-specific cell surface markers
